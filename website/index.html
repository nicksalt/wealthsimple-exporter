Export your Wealthsimple transactions.

A simple browser extension that adds a button to your sidebar to export transactions as CSV, OFX, or QFX. Runs entirely locally in your browser.

  • Injects directly into the UI (no new tab required)
  • Uses your active session (no relinking/login)
  • Format support for YNAB, Actual Budget, and Quicken
  • Security: zero data collection, local processing only
